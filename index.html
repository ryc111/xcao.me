The Natural Evolution

Engineer at Heart

I spent 6 years as a Software Engineer at Google and AWS. I wasn't just "closing tickets"—I found myself obsessing over the why behind every architectural decision. I realized that the best technical solutions are the ones users never notice. This led me to lead Living Room initiatives at YouTube, bridging the gap between deep backend systems and user-facing joy.

Product Strategist

Now, I leverage my technical fluency to drive product strategy. Instead of just looking at dashboards, I dig deep to uncover the human behaviors behind the data—like discovering why millions of users abandoned a screen due to a minor UI quirk. I build products by understanding market context and user friction, crafting seamless, cross-device journeys that naturally drive engagement.

Philosophy

"Building for Scale without losing the Soul."

My engineering background grounds me in the "how," while my product leadership drives the "why." "Building for scale" means architecting robust systems; "keeping the soul" means ensuring those systems never overshadow the user.

I believe the most powerful platforms are those that become invisible. My vision is to build next-generation platforms where the technology gets out of the way, allowing authentic human connection and immersive experiences to take center stage.
